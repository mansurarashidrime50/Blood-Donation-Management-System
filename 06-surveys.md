## About the Survey

| Detail | Info |
|--------|------|
| **Purpose** | Understand public interest in blood donation |

## Section 3: What People Want

### Features That Encourage Donation

| Feature | Percentage |
|---------|------------|
| Online registration | 85% |
| Appointment reminders | 78% |
| Mobile app | 70% |
| Donor rewards | 65% |
| Health tracking | 55% |

### Preferred Contact Method

| Method | Percentage |
|--------|------------|
| SMS | 45% |
| Email | 30% |
| WhatsApp | 20% |
| Phone call | 5% |

### Concerns About Donating

| Concern | Percentage |
|---------|------------|
| Health issues | 40% |
| Pain/discomfort | 25% |
| Time commitment | 20% |
| Privacy | 15% |

---

## Key Findings

| Finding | Result |
|---------|--------|
| High Interest | 60% have donated before |
| Convenience Matters | 85% want online registration |
| Communication | SMS is preferred (45%) |
| Main Barrier | Health concerns (40%) |
| Future Adoption | 80% would use digital system |

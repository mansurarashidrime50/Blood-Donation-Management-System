# Survey Documentation

## Survey Overview
- **Purpose**: Understand public interest in blood donation and identify user needs
- **Target Audience**: General public (potential donors, previous donors)
- **Sample Size**: 150 respondents
- **Response Rate**: 75%

## Survey Questions

### Section 1: Demographic Information
1. Age Range:
   - 18-25: 30% (45)
   - 26-35: 35% (53)
   - 36-50: 20% (30)
   - 50+: 15% (22)

2. Occupation:
   - Student: 25%
   - Professional: 40%
   - Self-employed: 20%
   - Other: 15%

### Section 2: Donation Experience
3. Have you ever donated blood?
   - Yes: 60% (90)
   - No: 40% (60)

4. If yes, how often?
   - Regularly: 15% (22)
   - Occasionally: 45% (68)
   - Once: 40% (60)

### Section 3: System Requirements
5. What features would encourage you to donate?
   - Online registration: 85%
   - Appointment reminders: 78%
   - Mobile app: 70%
   - Donor rewards: 65%
   - Health tracking: 55%

6. How would you prefer to be contacted?
   - SMS: 45%
   - Email: 30%
   - WhatsApp: 20%
   - Phone call: 5%

7. What are your concerns about donating?
   - Health issues: 40%
   - Pain/discomfort: 25%
   - Time commitment: 20%
   - Privacy: 15%

## Key Findings
1. **High Interest**: 60% have donated blood before
2. **Convenience Matters**: 85% want online registration
3. **Communication Preference**: SMS is the preferred method (45%)
4. **Main Barrier**: Health concerns (40%)
5. **Future Adoption**: 80% would use a digital system

## Recommendations
1. Implement easy online registration
2. Provide appointment reminders via SMS
3. Create mobile-friendly interface
4. Address health concerns with information
5. Offer donor rewards program
6. Ensure privacy and data security

# Blood Donation Management System

## The Problem

People in Bangladesh are dying because they cannot find blood in time.

---

## Who Suffers?

### Poor Patients
- Cannot afford to buy blood
- Don't have smartphones
- Don't understand English
- Don't know where to go

### Hospitals
- Cannot find donors quickly
- No blood inventory system
- No emergency alerts

### Donors
- Want to help but don't know when
- No notification system
- No nearby donation centers

### Blood Banks
- Blood expires without tracking
- No real-time inventory
- No connection with hospitals

---

## Why Is This Happening?

| Problem | Reason |
|---------|--------|
| No central donor list | No digital system exists |
| No urgent alerts | No SMS system |
| Blood is expensive | Private banks charge high prices |
| Only English | Poor people don't understand |
| No internet | 60% have no smartphones |
| No hospital connection | Hospitals work alone |

---

## How Bad Is It?

| Fact | Number |
|------|--------|
| Deaths per year | 10,000+ |
| Emergency cases | 70% |
| No smartphone | 60% |
| Can't afford blood | 80% |
| Blood wasted | 40% |

---

# The Solution

## What We Will Build

**BloodConnect** is a free and simple blood donation platform for poor people in Bangladesh.

---

## Key Features

### 1. Donor Registration
- Register using phone number
- Provide name, blood group, location
- No documents needed
- No internet required

### 2. Blood Request
- Hospitals can request blood
- Specify blood group and quantity
- Request sent to nearby donors

### 3. SMS Alerts
- Donors receive SMS when blood is needed
- No internet needed
- Quick response

### 4. Bengali Language
- Everything in Bengali
- SMS in Bengali
- Everyone can understand

### 5. Free Service
- No cost for donors
- No cost for patients
- Free blood for poor people

### 6. Hospital Dashboard
- Track blood inventory
- See available donors
- Manage requests

### 7. Blood Bank Management
- Update stock
- Get expiry alerts
- Reduce wastage

---

## How It Works

### For Donors
1. Register with phone
2. Get SMS when blood is needed
3. Go to hospital and donate
4. Get thank you SMS

### For Patients
1. Request blood through hospital
2. System finds matching donors
3. Donors come to donate
4. Patient gets free blood

### For Hospitals
1. Request blood when needed
2. System sends SMS alerts
3. Track incoming donors
4. Update inventory

### For Blood Banks
1. Update stock levels
2. System tracks expiry dates
3. Get alerts for low stock
4. Manage inventory better
